Poker has been played for over a hundred years and has been growing in popularity quickly over the past few decades. Texas Hold'em is the most popular among the current top players. There's always a poker tournament going on somewhere in the world. Even ESPN shows the final rounds of the world series of poker.

On this website you will find all the necessary information to start playing poker yourselves and start up a local poker club if you wish. Enjoy the game and may the odds be ever in your favour.
